About Tjuvkil Paddle

Welcome to Tjuvkil Paddle, your destination for an unforgettable water experience in Tjuvkil Harbor. We offer rentals of sea kayaks and stand-up paddleboards (SUP) for all skill levels. At Tjuvkil Paddle, we focus on a personal experience, prioritizing quality over quantity. Currently, we offer daily rentals so you can explore the beautiful coastline at your own pace. Our passionate team is dedicated to sharing the beauty of Tjuvkil and the surrounding waters. Join us for a day on the water and enjoy the tranquility of the harbor, whether you're seeking relaxation or adventure.
